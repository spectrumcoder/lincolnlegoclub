Events

Kid's & Teen's Night

On Kids's and Teen's Night, kids have the ability to collaborate with peers their own age and ability. Kids and Teens on Monday - Thursday from 4 PM to 7 PM.

Adult's Night

On Adult's Night, adults have the ability to meet with others to build intricate lego sculptures and sets. Adults meet on Tuesday - Friday from 6 PM to 9 PM.

Theme Night

Join us on Theme Night to build theme related lego sets. Themes include; Star Wars, Ocean, Masters, and Color only themes. Theme Nights occur on Friday - Sunday from 4 PM to 9 PM.
